Truest Token’s decentralized messaging app

TruChat

Buy, sell, receive and securely store cryptocurrencies with Truest Token’s native decentralized messaging app. Designed to remove the middleman and help you realize greater returns on your investment, TruChat boasts a secure system and end-to-end encryption that ensures your messages remain private and your assets are safe at all times. A built-in secure crypto wallet lets you receive cryptocurrencies from all over the world and can only be accessed with a unique key.

But TruChat is more than just a cryptocurrency exchange or wallet: it’s a community and a specialized messaging app that lets you connect with others who share your passion for cryptocurrency. Complete transactions while chatting up with your friends and peers from every point around the globe.

How it works

TruChat is completely secure, with anonymous sign-up that only requires a crypto wallet address. This ensures all of your sensitive personal information such as your name, ID, or phone number, stays safe while providing you with the ultimate user privacy. The state-of-the-art end-to-end encryption protects your private messages, eliminating any risk of hacks or data leaks, and securing your messaging history and exchanged files.

What’s more, because of the way TruChat was designed, it completely revolutionizes the way you sell, buy and exchange crypto assets. Trade Tokens without the need for an external exchange in a seamless, hassle-free, completely secure system that makes trading as easy as sending a message.
