Global Charity

At Truest Token, we believe in giving back to the community and supporting those less fortunate. That’s why we have created Global Charity: Truest’s native charity program that will partner up with global foundations that are in direct contact with the less privileged and those in need to change lives for the better.

Cryptocurrencies present major opportunities for charities around the world, making it easier for nonprofit organizations to receive aid money and distribute them to where they’re most needed, as well as ensuring greater transparency for any donated funds with the help of the blockchain. At Truest Token, we believe cryptocurrency is here to stay and we are excited to leverage its power for good, helping those less fortunate around the world and improving lives.

We carefully vet and pre-select the non-profit organizations we partner with to ensure anyone participating in the Truest Global Charity is confident about the projects we support. Our ultimate goal is to change lives, provide aid and educate individuals around the globe about the opportunities of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.
