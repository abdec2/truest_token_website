The Truest Token Ecosystem

The Truest Token Ecosystem continuously generates funds, supporting the value growth of the Truest Token—and your investment. Here is how it works:

Truest-NFTs

NFTs, or non-fungible tokens, are digital assets that represent media objects such as art, texts, music, in-game items and more. Each NFT is unique and cannot be replaced or replicated, which inherently gives them value. As part of the Truest Token Ecosystem, we have developed Truest NFTs which Truest Token holders can buy and sell, supporting artists and creators while growing their wealth. The Truest NFTs are designed and created with value to generate continuous revenue that can be used to buy back and burn Truest Tokens, creating even more value for the project.

Truest Marketplace

Sell and buy with confidence at the Truest Marketplace: a unique trading platform designed as part of the Truest Ecosystem that allows you to sell and buy merchandise all around the world. Truest Token holders can buy products from any point around the globe, or sell their products on the native marketplace. The revenue will then be used to buy back and burn Truest Tokens, taking them out of the circulation which increases each token’s value.

At Truest Token, we believe in giving back to the community and we do so through our unique Truest Global Charity program. Find out more about how it works here.

Truest Projects

Truest Token was designed as a community-centered token, helping our token holders not only buy and sell merchandise and NFTs but achieve financial freedom by investing in a future-proofed project with ensured longevity. To generate cash inflow and sustain, maintain and enhance project development, we rely on a range of Truest projects. The funds from each project will be used to buy back and burn Truest Tokens and shared amongst the holders and investors. This helps us burn a massive number of tokens and ensure that the token’s value follows a continuous upward trajectory.

TruChat

Connect with others, securely send, buy, receive and store cryptocurrency with TruChat, Truest Token’s native decentralized messaging app. End-to-end encryption ensures all your messages are private and your assets are secure at all times. Complete transactions while chatting with peers from all over the world and meet like-minded individuals who are just as passionate about crypto and Truest Token as you are! The TruChat app comes with a secure built-in crypto wallet that can be accessed only by the user who holds the unique key. Learn more about TruChat here.

The TruVerse

Join the future: join the metaverse. The TruVerse is Truest Token’s native meta-universe, a virtual environment where Truest Token holders can live, play and connect with each other. Designed to parallel the real world, the TruVerse will allow you to meet and connect with others, buy land, houses and advertisement space and create your dream virtual life. Create your virtual persona with custom avatars, and generate revenue by offering exclusive avatars or items as NFTs. Any revenue generated from the TruVerse will be used to buy and burn Truest Tokens, inherently increasing its value with every purchase.
